Gender Diversity

Gender equality policies at MPGI have been brought into action with the formation of women helpline & women empowerment committee. At MPGI we constantly try to get conducive ecosystem for fair representation of all genders.

Either they be our girl students, female faculty & staff members and for every other gender. Our HR policies are framed to bring justice for accrued talent without any assumption or prejudices related to gender. Gender neutrality is considered as a pre-requisite for all our open positions. We strive to impart and inculcate behavioral discipline amongst our students towards all genders to create better future workplaces.
